    UI/UX - Mobile Developement

    PocketCare — Health Records Platform

    PocketCare is a digital health records platform designed to help users securely store, view, and understand their medical data. The product focuses on simplifying complex health information through intuitive interfaces and AI-powered insights, enabling users to make informed decisions about their health.

    My Role & Activities

    • Led branding, visual identity, and end-to-end UI/UX for web and mobile experiences.
    • Designed a health summary dashboard that presents AI-generated insights from medical reports in a clear, actionable format.
    • Created the UX for an AI health companion chatbot, allowing users to ask questions, understand reports, and navigate health data more easily.
    • Designed user flows for record upload, categorization, viewing, and secure sharing.
    • Built reusable UI components and design systems to ensure consistency and scalability.
    • Collaborated closely with product, engineering, and backend teams to refine workflows and feature logic.
    • Focused on accessibility, clarity, and trust—critical for healthcare-related experiences.

    Tools & Technologies

    • Design: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
    • Frontend: React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    • Prototyping & Interaction: Figma Prototypes
    • AI UX: Conversational flow design, prompt structuring, AI insight visualization
    • Collaboration: Git, Jira, Agile workflows

About Me

Beyond the Screen

I’m someone who enjoys bringing structure to complexity — whether it’s designing intuitive digital products or composing music. As a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Engineer, I’m naturally curious, detail-oriented, and driven by how people experience technology in real life. I care deeply about clarity, accessibility, and thoughtful interaction design.

Outside of product work, I’m a professional musician with over 12 years of performance experience, which strongly influences my sense of rhythm, timing, and storytelling in design. I enjoy exploring new tools, experimenting with AI-driven experiences, and continuously refining my craft through learning, collaboration, and creative problem-solving.
